# Category Suite Plan Template

For individual app SKUs within a category suite.

**Strategy:** Each Plan = Starting Point + Expansion Path. Emphasize business scenarios over technical specs.

---

## PLAN NAME (3-5 words)

**Format:** `[App Name] [Category] Plan`

**Example:** `WordPress CMS Plan`

---

## PLAN SUMMARY (50-80 chars)

**Format:** `Deploy [App] for [use case], expand to [Category] apps as needs grow`

**Example:** `Deploy WordPress for content management, expand to CMS apps as needs grow`

---

## PLAN DESCRIPTION

**Note:** Use `html-plan-category-suite-app.html` for actual generation.

### Structure:
1. **Target + Growth Scenario:** For [persona] who will need [business evolution scenario]
2. **What You Deploy Today:** [App Name] pre-configured for [primary use case]
3. **Natural Next Steps:** Common expansion paths based on business triggers
   - Add [App 2] when [scenario]
   - Expand to [App 3] for [requirement]
4. **What You Get:** Deployment, Websoft9 unified management, backups, updates, support
5. **Billing Clarity:** Billed per-app. This plan includes [Primary App], add others anytime.
6. **Time to Value:** Deploy in [X] minutes, expand in 5 minutes

**Example:**
```
For marketing teams managing websites who will likely need training systems and e-commerce capabilities as they grow.

Deploy WordPress today for content management and blogging. When your business evolves:
- Add Moodle in Q2 when launching employee/customer training
- Expand to Magento in Q3 for online store launch
- Include Matomo for unified analytics across all properties

What You Get: Pre-configured WordPress, Websoft9 control panel, automated backups, one-click updates, 24/7 support.
```
