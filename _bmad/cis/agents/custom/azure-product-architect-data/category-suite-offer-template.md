# Category Suite Offer Template

For multi-app category suites (e.g., "DevOps Suite", "CMS Suite").

**Strategy:** Growth Infrastructure Platform - Position as enabling business evolution through scalable multi-app deployment. Lead with primary app, articulate expansion flexibility.

---

## TITLE

**Format:** `[Primary App] Hosting with Multi-App [Category] Platform`

**Example:** `WordPress Hosting with Multi-App CMS Platform`

**Rationale:** Captures specific app search traffic (e.g., "WordPress Azure") while signaling platform flexibility

---

## SUMMARY (150-200 chars)

**Format:** `Start with [Primary App], scale to [Secondary Apps]. Deploy in minutes, expand without rebuilding. [Category] platform built for business growth.`

**Key:** Lead with high-traffic app, emphasize "start → expand" narrative, include expansion benefit

**Example:** `Start with WordPress, scale to 10+ apps. Deploy in minutes, expand without rebuilding. CMS platform built for business growth.`

---

## SHORT DESCRIPTION (200-250 chars)

**Format:** `Deploy [Primary App] today, add [Category Apps List] as your business evolves. Pre-configured platform with unified management. Expand in minutes, not weeks.`

**Example:** `Deploy WordPress today, add Drupal, Joomla, Moodle as your business evolves. Pre-configured CMS platform with unified management. Expand in minutes, not weeks.`
