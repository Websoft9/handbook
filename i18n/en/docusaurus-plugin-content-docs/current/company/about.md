# About the Company

## Introduction

[Websoft9](https://www.websoft9.com/about-us) is a software supply chain operation service provider based on cloud computing and open source. We serve as a bridge between cloud platforms, software vendors, integrators, and users.

Websoft9 integrates open source and AI technologies to intelligently select and integrate thousands of popular cloud-native applications. We create a comprehensive product catalog covering infrastructure, middleware, containers, databases, DevOps, cloud-native applications, enterprise management, enterprise intelligence, and IoT scenarios, forming one-stop solutions. Together with our partners, we serve over 30 million cloud enterprise customers across more than 140 countries worldwide.

Websoft9 has a comprehensive cloud-native enablement system, providing global, online, professional, and intelligent software integration and services. With these technologies, partners can build scenario-based solutions for their target customers like building blocks, helping customers successfully adapt to business globalization and digitalization.

Currently, our ecosystem partners include:

- Cloud Platforms: Azure, AWS, Google Cloud, Alibaba Cloud, Tencent Cloud, Huawei Cloud, etc.
- Software Vendors: GitLab, WordPress, Odoo, ERPNext, Mingdao, HighGo, Drupal, etc.
- Resellers: CEC, Chinasoft International, Taijitech, etc.

Our customers are located worldwide, from individual makers to Global 500 companies, including: ABB, Adobe, Deloitte, Kingdee, Dell, Siemens, Mengniu, Volkswagen, Peking University, Sprout Foundation, etc.
